About

Wolfgang's Steakhouse is a steakhouse whose flagship restaurant is located on Park Avenue in Manhattan. The restaurant is owned by former headwaiter at Peter Luger Steak House, Wolfgang Zwiener. Wolfgang's has been frequently ranked as among the top ten steakhouses in New York City.[1] The atmosphere has been described as "more efficient than warm", by Frank Bruni of the New York Times.

  • Establish: 2004
  • Current Owner: Wolfgang Zwiener
  • Head Chef: Wolfgang Zwiener
  • Food Type: Steakhouse
  • Street Address: 4 Park Ave
Porterhouse

Almost 5 stars because the service is unparalleled! If I dropped my napkin someone was there to replace it before I even noticed it fell. Super sharp,Attentive and charming. The bottle of flowers red wine was lovely! Expensive but wonderful. My only negative comments is that the lump crab app didn't taste too fresh or impressive and the side of creamed spinach was too salty! I would skip seafood here and get to the meat of the matter ;) shared the porterhouse for two- holy moly is that thing huge!!!!! But it's probably the best, most flavorful, tender and perfectly cooked steak I have had in ages! Superb! The leftovers were amazing with my steak and eggs at home too! Every bite of steak gets better and better! Come here if carnivorous with a good wine palate.
